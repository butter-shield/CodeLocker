Getting schema from DB and making context from DB
dotnet ef dbcontext scaffold "Server=localhost\SQLEXPRESS;Database=ChickenCoupDB_Dev;Trusted_Connection=True;TrustServerCertificate=True;" Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.SqlServer --output-dir Models --context AppDbContext --force

** Updating from app to DB
dotnet ef migrations add InitialCreate8
dotnet ef database update
